# Cover Letter: ZKM Rauschenberg Residency

Dear ZKM Selection Committee,

I am writing to express my strong interest in the Rauschenberg Residency at ZKM. My practice, centered on the **ORGANVM System**, operates at the intersection of systems engineering, media theory, and institutional critique. 

As a developer who treats "Governance as Art," I have spent the last several years building a 113-repository computational infrastructure designed to sustain complex creative production. This system coordinates the lifecycle of ideas from abstract theory to public manifestation, utilizing machine-readable registries and formal state machines to manage over 810,000 words of documentation and 94 active CI/CD pipelines.

The Rauschenberg Residency represents a unique opportunity to transition this internal infrastructure into a public-facing media installation. At ZKM, I intend to focus on "Organ-VI: Koinonia" (Community), developing interactive interfaces that allow audiences to "probe" the system and participate in the performative act of modifying its machine-readable constitution.

By bringing the ORGANVM system to ZKM, I seek to explore how private systems of governance can be rendered transparent and interactive, transforming the administrative and technical orchestration of creative labor into a site of collaborative aesthetic inquiry.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

Anthony James Padavano
