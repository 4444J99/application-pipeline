# Methodology: Founder Sustainability Protocol

## One-Line
Systematic infrastructure for sustained independent production — scheduled recovery, peer accountability, and professional support as non-negotiable operating requirements.

## Short (150 words)
Solo practitioners face a 73% burnout prevalence rate. The ORGANVM system treats sustainability as an engineering constraint, not an aspiration. Three structural mechanisms prevent degradation:

**Structured breaks.** Calendar-blocked recovery periods are non-negotiable. Development sprints (33 completed) include explicit wind-down phases. The sprint retrospective format enforces reflection before the next cycle begins.

**Peer accountability.** ORGAN-VI (Koinonia) operates reading groups and community channels that provide external perspective on pace and scope. These function as informal advisory boards — peers who see the work and flag unsustainable patterns before they compound.

**Professional support.** Proactive engagement with coaching and therapeutic support as preventive infrastructure, not crisis response. The same systematic thinking applied to 103 repositories applies to personal capacity management.

## Key Metric
33 named sprints completed with documented retrospectives — sustained production over years, not months.
