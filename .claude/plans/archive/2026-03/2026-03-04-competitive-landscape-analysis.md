**Date:** 2026-03-04
**Type:** Research / Literature Review
**Scope:** Doctoral-grade competitive landscape analysis across 7 categories

## Summary

Comprehensive analysis of 60+ products, platforms, and academic systems that overlap with the custom-built application-pipeline system. Covers commercial ATS/CRM tools, AI job matching, academic MCDA research, grant management software, relationship CRMs, open-source systems, and decision support theory.

## Key Finding

No existing system combines all of: multi-dimensional weighted scoring, pipeline state machine, relationship decay modeling, multi-track support (jobs + grants + residencies), ATS API integration, reachability analysis, mode switching, and block-based content composition. The custom system occupies a unique intersection.
